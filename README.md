## 👋 Hey there, meet Eligify!

Ever tried to find out which government schemes, scholarships, or subsidies you qualify for? It usually means diving into confusing websites, reading through 20-page PDF documents, and leaving with more questions than answers. 

**We built Eligify to fix that.** 

Eligify is a friendly, guided web platform that turns a complicated, frustrating process into something simple. We help bridge the massive gap between the support that is *available* and the people who *actually use it*.

---

## 🛑 The Problem

Government schemes are meant to help people, but in reality:
- 🧩 Information is scattered across dozens of different websites.
- 📜 Eligibility criteria are written in complex jargon.
- 🤷 People don't know where to start searching.
- 😔 Millions miss out on benefits they rightfully deserve.

## 💡 Our Solution

Eligify does the heavy lifting for you. Instead of searching manually, you just:
1. Answer a few simple questions in our guided questionnaire.
2. Or, just type what you need naturally (e.g., *"I am a 2nd-year college student from Haryana looking for a scholarship"*).

✨ **Boom!** You instantly get a personalized list of schemes you *actually* qualify for.

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

### 1️⃣ Smart Eligibility Flow
Tell us a bit about yourself—like your state, income range, and occupation. Our system instantly cross-references your profile against our database of scheme rules.

### 2️⃣ Personalized Results
No more overwhelming lists. Eligify filters out the noise, prioritizing the schemes that match your profile perfectly. 

### 3️⃣ Crystal Clear Breakdowns
We translate government jargon into plain English. For every scheme, you'll clearly see:
- **Who** can apply.
- **What** the benefits are.
- **Which** documents you need.
- **How** to apply, step-by-step.

### 4️⃣ AI-Powered Assistance
We use AI where it makes a real difference—like converting your natural language questions into search filters and summarizing long, boring government descriptions.

## 💻 What's Under the Hood?

We chose a modern, fast, and scalable tech stack:

- **Frontend:** React + Vite (for a blazing-fast UI)
- **Styling:** Custom CSS + CSS Modules
- **Backend:** Node.js & Express.js
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **AI Brain:** OpenAI API

*(Note: The current iteration focuses heavily on the React frontend and local data matching to ensure a smooth, instant user experience).*

---

## 🚀 Current Project Scope

Right now, Eligify features:
- ✅ A beautifully designed, interactive eligibility questionnaire.
- ✅ A curated database of real government schemes.
- ✅ A robust, rule-based matching engine.
- ✅ A clean, responsive, and accessible user interface.
- ✅ The ability to save and bookmark your favorite schemes.

Our goal is to increase awareness, reduce confusion, and ultimately empower people to take action and claim the support they deserve.
